# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

**Author:** Kumar Ansh Prasad  

---

##  Project Overview  

This project analyzes customer churn and retention patterns in a subscription-based business using real-world customer data.  

The objective is to identify key churn drivers, high-risk customer segments, and actionable strategies to improve customer retention and lifetime value.

---

##  Objectives  

- Calculate overall churn rate  
- Analyze churn by contract type  
- Study customer tenure impact on retention  
- Evaluate pricing influence on churn  
- Identify payment method risk factors  
- Detect high-risk customer segments  
- Provide business-focused recommendations  

---

##  Tools & Technologies Used  

- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

##  Key Insights  

- Approximately **26–27% customers churned**.  
- Month-to-month contracts show significantly higher churn rates.  
- Customers within the first 6 months are most likely to churn.  
- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn.  
- Electronic check users demonstrate higher churn compared to auto-payment users.  

---

##  Business Recommendations  

1. Encourage long-term contracts with discounted annual plans.  
2. Improve onboarding experience for new customers (0–6 months).  
3. Provide loyalty incentives for high-paying customers.  
4. Promote auto-payment methods to reduce friction.  
5. Implement early churn-risk detection campaigns.
